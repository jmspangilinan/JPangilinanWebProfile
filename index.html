Thesis Capstone

The Biodegradable Waste Filtration and Compostable Plastic Conversion machine transforms compostable waste into biodegradable plastic using common kitchen ingredients like potato starch, coconut oil, vinegar, glycerol, and water. Powered by an Arduino microcontroller, the machine employs servo and stepper motors to achieve precise timing, allowing each step in the process to be completed accurately for optimal results.
